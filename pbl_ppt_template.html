RAही

One Stop Community for All Travellers

"RAही (Raahi) means traveler in Hindi. A community-driven travel social media platform that integrates journey tracking, social interaction, personalized recommendations, and gamification into a single space for Indian travelers."

Introduction & Vision

RAही connects people who enjoy discovering new places and offbeat destinations, combining travel planning tools with a social media layer to foster engagement. Current apps are fragmented — they focus on single aspects like booking (MakeMyTrip), navigation (Google Maps), or reviews (TripAdvisor). Travelers lack an integrated space to exchange real-time tips and itineraries.

Enhance Travel

Interactive maps & user-generated itineraries

Promote Engagement

Social interactivity & gamified challenges

Empower Guides

Monetization for guides & photographers

Problem Statement

  • ▸App Fragmentation: Current apps focus on single aspects — booking (MakeMyTrip), navigation (Google Maps), or reviews (TripAdvisor). No unified platform exists.
  • ▸No Real-Time Exchange: Travelers lack an integrated space to exchange real-time tips, itineraries, and community-driven recommendations.
  • ▸Missing Social Layer: Existing travel tools lack social engagement — no community feeds, gamified challenges, or interactive content sharing.
  • ▸No Journey Tracking: No platform automates the tracking of geographical milestones or visualizes a traveler's journey history on a map.

Scope — In

  • ▸ Destination discovery by theme (beaches, culture, food)
  • ▸ Interactive map-based exploration & route planning
  • ▸ Story posting (photo + caption) & social features
  • ▸ Mobile-first, responsive UI

Scope — Out

  • ▸ Real-time booking (hotels, activities)
  • ▸ Offline mode & GPS tracking
  • ▸ Multilingual support beyond English/Hindi

Key Innovations

Destination Discovery

Browse destinations by theme — beaches, culture, food, nature — with tag-based filtering and curation.

Interactive Mapping

Mapbox-powered interactive maps with clickable markers and route planning for journey visualization.

Community Feed

Social feed with posts, likes, comments, and bookmarks — building a travel community, not just a tool.

Gamification Engine

Gamified challenges & achievements that turn travel milestones into social badges and rewards.

Functional Modules

Destination Discovery

  • ▸ Browse by tags: food, nature, culture, heritage
  • ▸ Curated recommendations per user interest
  • ▸ Theme-based destination filtering

Interactive Map

  • ▸ Clickable markers with destination info
  • ▸ Route planning & journey visualization
  • ▸ Map interaction response < 1 second

Community Feed

  • ▸ Social feed: posts, likes, comments, bookmarks
  • ▸ Story upload: 1 photo + caption (≤ 5MB)
  • ▸ Real-time updates via Supabase subscriptions

User Roles & Security

  • ▸ Admin: Moderate content, manage spam
  • ▸ End User: Discover, post, engage socially
  • ▸ JWT Auth, rate limiting, XSS/SQLi protection
  • ▸ WCAG 2.1 accessibility compliance

Lazy Loading & Caching

Lazy-loaded images with Cloudflare caching for optimal performance and fast page loads.

Error Handling

Defined mechanisms for Auth failure, API timeouts, 404 pages, and file size limit enforcement.

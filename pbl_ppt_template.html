RAही

Traveler's Social Network

"An aesthetic, mobile-first social networking platform for the modern Indian traveler — turning geography into social capital through GPS-driven analytics and gamified milestones."

Executive Summary

RAही addresses the gap in geographical progress tracking by allowing users to document journeys, track state-wise milestones (28 States + 8 UTs), and interact with a community of explorers. By combining real-time social logic with GPS-driven analytics, RAही turns travel into a gamified, social milestone — providing a functional, aesthetic, and high-performance solution for the modern Indian traveler.

Problem Statement

  • ▸Geographical Progress Tracking: Standard platforms like Instagram lack a specialized way for Indian travelers to track "State-wise" milestones.
  • ▸Content Fragmentation: Travelers struggle to maintain a consolidated record of journeys; photos remain scattered while notes are lost across apps.
  • ▸The "Zero-Media" Barrier: Platforms prioritize visuals, making text-only travel narratives feel secondary or aesthetically unappealing.
  • ▸Privacy vs. Discovery: No structured system balances public content discovery with a private follow-request model.

Market Research & Target Audience

Target: India's 18–35 age group — university students, solo travelers, and Gen Z/Millennials. A growing "State Collection" trend (visiting all 28 states) exists, yet no app automates tracking this goal.

Gap Analysis & Innovation

TripAdvisor focuses on reviews, Instagram on general visuals. RAही uniquely focuses on the "Path of the Traveler" through Indian geography — automated state-milestone tracking, hybrid content engine, and interactive social mapping.

Key Innovations

State-Milestone Engine

Auto cross-references check-ins with 28 States + 8 UTs for a real-time "States Covered" metric.

Hybrid Content Engine

Dynamic layout that restructures UI based on content type — expanding for photography, prioritizing captions for text stories.

Interactive Social Mapping

Merges social posts with GIS markers to create a personal travel map plotting user history visually.

Smart Travel Check-in

GPS-enabled reverse geocoding auto-suggests the correct Indian region during post creation.

Core Features

Auth & User Profiles

Content Creation & Feed

  • ▸ Photo only / Text only / Check-in only / Any combo

  • ▸ Real-time sync via onSnapshot listeners

Social Ecosystem

  • ▸ Follow / Request / Following states
  • ▸ Activity feed with aggregated interactions

  • ▸ Private profile mode for non-followers

Discovery & Mapping
